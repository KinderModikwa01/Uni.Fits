Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a product review?

You can submit a product review by filling out the form at the bottom of this page. Simply provide your name, email address, rating, and your review comments. All reviews are moderated before being published to ensure authenticity.

How long does it take for my review to appear?

We typically process and publish reviews within 2-3 business days. All reviews go through a verification process to ensure they meet our community guidelines.

Can I edit or remove my review after submitting?

Yes, if you need to edit or remove your review, please contact our customer service team at reviews@stylefix.com with your request and we'll be happy to assist you.

Why was my review not published?

Reviews may not be published if they contain inappropriate language, personal information, or if they don't relate to the actual product experience. We also don't publish reviews that appear to be fake or incentivized.

Do you respond to customer reviews?

Yes, we read and respond to reviews regularly, especially when customers have questions or concerns that we can address. We value all feedback as it helps us improve our products and services.
